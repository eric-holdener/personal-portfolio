A little about me...

Hi! My name is Eric, and I am an aspiring developer. I've been interested in programming for 5 years now, coding bits of little toys and scripts here and there for fun / to make my life easier. I never really thought I was cut out to be a programmer back then though, so I didn't pursue it any further. One day, something just clicked for me while I was working on a script, and I decided to pursue programming as a career. Since then, six months ago, I have spent 90% of my free hours dedicated to honing the craft, learning coding principles and full stack web development. I hope you like what you see, I've worked very hard on it.
